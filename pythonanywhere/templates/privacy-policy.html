Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2025

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Identifiers: Email address, name.
  • Google Calendar Data: Event titles, dates/times, attendees, reminders.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, device type, IP address.
  • Cookies & Tracking: Session cookies, analytics.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide & Improve: Sync calendars and optimize features.
  • Communicate: Account emails, feature announcements (opt‑out available).
  • Security & Compliance: Monitor for abuse, comply with legal requests.

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

  • With Consent: You explicitly allow a share.
  • Third‑Party Services: Google API per their Privacy Policy.
  • Legal: To comply with law enforcement or court orders.

4. Data Retention

We retain data while your account is active. You can delete your account anytime via Settings → Account → Delete.

5. Security

All data in transit is protected with SSL/TLS. Sensitive data at rest is encrypted. No system is foolproof, so remain vigilant with your credentials.

6. Your Choices

  • Access & Update: Edit profile/calendar connections in your Google account settings.
  • Opt‑Out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails via links in emails.
  • Revoke Calendar: Google Account → Security → Third‑party apps.

8. Changes to This Policy

We'll post a notice here and update the “Last updated” date for material changes.
